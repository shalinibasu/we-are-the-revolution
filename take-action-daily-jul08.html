EDUCATE.

Watch this short two minute video of Ella Baker delivering a speech in 1974.

Ella Baker addresses the importance of standing in solidarity.

"It takes organization. It takes dedication. It takes the willingness to stand by and do what has to be done, when it has to be done."

I urge you to listen to her speech and think about her powerful words as they are incredibly relevant today. And, as non-black allies, her words are important to contemplate when going forth with our activism and solidarity.
