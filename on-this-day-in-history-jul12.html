ON THIS DAY

On July 12th, 1864, George Washington Carver was born in Diamond, Missouri.

George Washington Carver was born to his enslaved parents. And like many enslaved children, his true birthdate won't be known, however this date is when it is speculated to be.

Carver is known for being an agricultural scientist as well as for his many inventions using the peanut.
